= Chart Panes

The link:{FinancialChartLink}.{FinancialChartName}.html[{FinancialChartName}] has multiple panes to display financial data. The panes for the {FinancialChartName} are the Price, Indicator, Volume and Zoom.

The following screenshot highlights the different panes:

pick:[wpf="image::images/financialchart_wpf_chartpanes.png[]"]
pick:[jquery="image::images/financialchart_jquery_chartpanes.png[]"]

=== Price Pane
The link:financial-chart-pane-price.html[Price Pane] renders prices using Line, Candlestick, Bar(OHLC), Column, trendlines and financial overlays.

=== Indicator Pane
The link:financial-chart-pane-indicator.html[Indicator Panes] renders all the financial indicators in a separate chart while the BollingerBands and PriceChannel overlays are rendered in the Price Pane because they share the same values range on Y-Axis.

=== Volume Pane
The link:financial-chart-pane-volume.html[Volume Pane] renders stocks volumes using Columns, Lines, and Area.

=== Zoom Pane
The link:financial-chart-pane-zoom.html[Zoom Pane] controls the zoom of all the panes.
